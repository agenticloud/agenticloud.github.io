How to Delete Your Data

This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data from FIA.

Option A — Delete from inside the mobile app (recommended)

  1. Open FIA on your phone and sign in.
  2. Go to Menu → Account → Settings.
  3. Tap Delete account & data, then confirm.

We will begin deleting or anonymising your account data. You’ll receive a confirmation once completed, except where limited retention is required by law (e.g., fraud prevention or tax records).

Option B — If you used Facebook Login

You can also remove our app from your Facebook account. This sends us a data-deletion request automatically.

  1. Open Facebook → Settings & privacy → Settings.
  2. Tap Apps and websites, choose FIA, then tap Remove.
  3. When prompted, allow Facebook to notify us about the removal so we can process deletion.

Option C — If you can’t access the app

Email us from the address linked to your account and request deletion:

  • Email: SUPPORT_EMAIL

Please include your full name and the email used in the app (and your Facebook app-scoped ID if known).

What gets deleted

Profile details, activity/history, and other data stored by FIA that is associated with your account will be deleted or anonymised. We do not control data held by Facebook—manage that via Facebook settings.

What may be retained

Minimal information may be kept where legally required or to resolve disputes and prevent abuse. Such data is limited and retained only for the necessary duration.

Important

  • Uninstalling the app does not delete your data—please use one of the options above.
  • Requests are processed promptly; completion times may vary depending on account state and legal obligations.
